Change Log

- Business entity was introduced and is a prerequisite for creating other entities (see no. 10)

- Introduction of auto-complete search ability for product, item, property and identifiers (see no. 11)

- Three layer authentication implementation for Application, Business and User. Hence, the following keys are now mandatory in the header request: ApplicationAuthorization, BusinessAuthorization and Authorization respectively. ApplicationAuthorization is hard-coded into each application release, BuisnessAuthorization is generated for a business during its registration/creation process. Authorization is generated whenever a users logs in. Also when a user logs in, the BusinessAuthorizations for the users businesses is appended to the users json object.

- Configurable Authorisation using a permutation of Application, Business and User

Business, roles, users, items, product, service
